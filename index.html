About Us

Our Goal

Our goal is to gain a strong position in local and international markets.

The Strength Behind Our Success

At the core of HESAM PAPER's success is a spirit of teamwork, commitment to quality, and a focus on sustainable development.

Our Mission

Producing the best and highest quality hard cardboard products to meet the needs of various industries such as packaging, printing and advertising is among our main goals.

Why Us?

HESAM PAPER was founded in 2019 to produce and supply various types of high-quality hard cardboard. We constantly strive to deliver sustainable and internationally compliant products using advanced technologies and top-grade raw materials.
